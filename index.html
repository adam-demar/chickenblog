Soul Kitchen

Today I discovered an absolute gem tucked away in a small corner deli. This crispy chicken sandwich featured a perfectly breaded cutlet with a golden-brown exterior that shattered with each bite. The chicken was juicy and tender inside, seasoned with just the right amount of salt and pepper. Topped with crisp lettuce, ripe tomato, and a tangy mayo-based sauce, all nestled between a soft brioche bun. This is what chicken sandwich dreams are made of!

The Spicy Sensation

If you love heat, this is your sandwich! The spicy marinade penetrated deep into the chicken breast, creating layers of flavor that built with each bite. A coating of cayenne-spiked breading added extra kick, while pickles and a cooling ranch drizzle provided the perfect balance. The potato bun held up beautifully against all that moisture and spice. My lips are still tingling, and I'm already planning my return visit!
